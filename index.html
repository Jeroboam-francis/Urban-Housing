About Us

Urban Haven Apartments offers premier living spaces located in the heart of Murang'a, Kiharu. We provide a variety of comfortable and affordable housing options, ranging from cozy single rooms to spacious two-bedroom self-contained apartments, catering to individuals, couples, and families alike.

Our mission is to create a community-oriented environment where residents can enjoy modern amenities, convenient access to services, and a vibrant living experience. Whether you're looking for a temporary residence or a long-term home, Urban Haven Apartments is committed to exceeding your expectations.
